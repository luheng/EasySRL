Can you answer the question using information in the sentence?

Overview

We want to teach our smart robot to understand human language and we need your help on this! The robot asks questions when it’s confused about information in the sentence. For example, given the sentence:

Stockholders who took advices from experts escaped the October debacle.

The robot will ask:

What escaped the October debacle?

and will represent you with the following choices:

  1. stockholders
  2. experts
  3. Answer not listed.
  4. Question is not understandable. 

what you need to do is to choose the correct answer -“1. stockholders”.

About Reading Questions

The robot-generated questions are not perfect. There are sometimes mistakes in tense, capitalization and choice of words (i.e. "who" vs. "what"). However, if you think the question is completely not understandable/ungrammatical, or not answerable given the sentence, please choose the option “Question is not valid”. 

For example, given sentence:

They included free checking, a credit-card, and self-deposit travelers checks for only $3 a month.

Examples of valid questions are (but not limited to):

What did they include?
What would they do for $3 a month?

However, the following question:

What would safe-deposit travelers checks?

is not valid.

About Choosing from Answers

Sometimes there are multiple answer to a question, and those are split with a bold & symbol. For example, given the question:

What did they include?

the answer:

free checking & a credit-card & self-deposit travelers checks

is a better answer over the following candidates:

free checking, a credit-card and self-deposit travelers checks
free checking & a credit-card

If you think there is a correct answer to the question, but that answer is not listed in the options, please go with the option "Answer is not listed".
