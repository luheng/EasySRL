Reading Questions

The robot-generated questions are not perfect. There are sometimes mistakes in tense, capitalization and choice of words (e.g., "who" vs. "what", "someone" vs. "something", or forgetting a "not"). In case of these minor errors, please try your best to give the correct answer. However, if you think the question is nonsensical, or is not answerable given the sentence, please choose the option Question is not valid. 

For example, given the sentence:

They included free checking, a safe-deposit box and travelers checks for only $3 a month.

Examples of valid questions include (but are not limited to):

What did they include?
What would they do for $3 a month?

However, the following question:

What would safe-deposit travelers checks?

is not valid. Furthermore, the question

What would travelers checks be for?

is not valid, even though it is grammatical, because it's not answered directly by the sentence. As another example, consider the sentence:

The company said it expects to complete the transaction by year end.

The question

What would the transaction be by?

is not valid. Even though the question may sound grammatical, and the sentence does implicitly suggest that the transaction would take place by year end, the phrase "by year end" in the sentence only explicitly refers to completing the transaction. So examples of acceptable questions include:

What would it complete the transaction by?

with the answer, e.g., year end, and

What would it do by year end?

with the answer complete the transaction.

Choosing the Best Answer

There may be multiple answer options that are related to the question, but you need to choose the one that most accurately answers the question. For example, given the sentence:

Japan 's domestic sales of cars in October rose 18% from a year earlier to 500,004 units.

and the question:

What rose?

Correct answers would include: "Japan 's domestic sales of cars", "domestic sales", "sales". Wrong answers would include: "Japan",  "Japan 's domestic", "cars". Usually there is only one correct answer among listed options. When there are multiple correct answers, please choose the shortest correct one, such as:

  1. Japan's domestic sales of cars
  2. domestic sales
  3. cars
  4. Answer not listed
  5. Question is not valid

Sometimes a question has multiple answers. These will be separated by a bold & symbol within the answer choices. For example, given the sentence:

They included free checking, a safe-deposit box and travelers checks for only $3 a month.

and the question:

What did they include?

the following answer:

free checking & a safe-deposit box & travelers checks

is a better answer than the other candidates such as:

free checking, a safe-deposit box and travelers checks
free checking & a safe-deposit box

If you think there is a correct answer to the question, but none of the listed ones is acceptable, please go with the option Answer is not listed.
