Can you answer the question using information in the sentence?

Overview

We want to teach our smart robot to understand human language and we need your help on this! The robot will ask you questions when it’s confused about the meaning or the structure of a sentence. For example, given the sentence:

Stockholders who took advices from experts escaped the October debacle.

The robot might ask:

What escaped the October debacle?

along with the following options as possible answers:

  1. stockholders
  2. experts
  3. Answer not listed.
  4. Question is not understandable. 

what you need to do is to choose the correct answer -“1. stockholders”.

About Reading Questions

The robot-generated questions are not perfect. There are sometimes mistakes in tense, capitalization and choice of words (i.e. "who" vs. "what", "someone" vs. "something"). Please try your best to give the correct answer. However, if you think the question is completely not understandable, or is not answerable given the sentence, please choose the option “Question is not valid”. 

For example, given the sentence:

They included free checking, a credit-card, and self-deposit travelers checks for only $3 a month.

Examples of valid questions are (but not limited to):

What did they include?
What would they do for $3 a month?

However, the following question:

What would safe-deposit travelers checks?

is not valid.

About Choosing the Best Answer

Sometimes there are multiple answers to a question, and those listed with a bold & symbol in between. For example, given the same sentence

They included free checking, a credit-card, and self-deposit travelers checks for only $3 a month.

 and the question:

What did they include?

the following answer:

free checking & a credit-card & self-deposit travelers checks

is a better answer then the other candidates such as:

free checking, a credit-card and self-deposit travelers checks
free checking & a credit-card

If you think there is a correct answer to the question, but that answer is not listed in the options, please go with the option "Answer is not listed".
